🔒 Your files never leave your device

Every tool on this site runs entirely inside your browser using standard Web APIs. Nothing is sent to any server, logged, stored, or shared.

  • All processing happens locally in your browser — no server involved.
  • Files are read directly from your disk and never transmitted over the network.
  • No cookies, no analytics, no accounts required.
  • You can even use these tools offline once the page has loaded.
